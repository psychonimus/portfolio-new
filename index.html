My Background

Growing up in the Historic city of India, Burhanpur M.P. my household was a vibrant blend of creativity and analytical thinking. From an early age, my profound love for both Computers and Creativity played a pivotal role in shaping my Future Interests.

The spark for my Tech fascination ignited at the tender age of ten, courtesy of my father's Nokia 2690. Although I pursued a degree in Computer Engineering, my heart truly resonates with the transformative influence of eye pleasing design craftsmanship. This passion is deeply rooted in my love for creative pursuits such as editing, photography, and technology.

Driven by curiosity, I found myself pondering: could I too create something capable of evoking such profound effects on people?

Today, I apply my multidisciplinary design skills, aiming to create solutions that make people feel and remember, elevating everyday experiences into inspiring solutions. Today, I channel my multidisciplinary Design and Development skills towards crafting solutions that resonate with people, leaving a lasting impression and transforming ordinary experiences into inspiring solutions.
